Higher Education: My pursuit of knowledge led me to The Brigham Young University,Idaho, where I majored in Computer Information Technology. The university experience was transformative, providing me with a wealth of knowledge and life lessons.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. I hope my experiences inspire and motivate others to embrace the joys of lifelong learning.
